PawPath 🐾
An AI-powered dog health and wellness tracker that monitors exercise, nutrition, and veterinary care — all personalized to your dog's breed, size, and needs.

What It Does
PawPath is a health tracker built for dogs. Think of it as a Fitbit or MyFitnessPal, but for your pup. Snap a photo to identify your dog's breed and estimated weight, upload vet records to build a complete health profile, and get personalized walking routes and schedules informed by real-time weather and air quality data.
Key Features

AI Breed & Weight Recognition — Upload a photo and Gemini AI identifies the breed and estimates weight, forming the foundation of your dog's health profile.
Dog Health Profiles — Track weight, age, breed, dietary needs, medications, and more in one place.
Vet Record Upload — Upload and store vaccination records, checkup notes, and medical history directly to your dog's profile.
Smart Walk Planning — Google Maps API generates walking routes tailored to your dog's exercise needs, breed characteristics, and location.
Weather-Aware Scheduling — Real-time weather and air quality data recommends optimal walk times, flagging extreme heat, cold, or poor conditions for your specific breed.
Breed-Specific Guidance — Adjusts exercise and care recommendations based on breed traits (e.g., shorter walks in heat for brachycephalic breeds, longer routes for high-energy breeds).
